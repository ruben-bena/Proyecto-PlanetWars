Project History

Planet Wars is a turn-based strategy game inspired by the popular browser game OGame. Developed as a final project for the first year of the Multiplatform Application Development vocational studies program, the game challenges players to manage their own planet, build a space fleet, and defend against enemy invasions while strategically expanding their empire.

In Planet Wars, players start with a fledgling planet and a small army of spaceships. The goal is to gather resources (metal and deuterium), upgrade technologies, and build a powerful fleet to fend off enemy attacks or launch invasions of other planets.

Planet Wars successfully combines strategic gameplay with technical execution, showcasing the team's ability to design, develop, and debug a functional application. The project highlights our proficiency in Java, database management, and web development while adhering to a tight deadline. Future improvements could include 3D graphics or multiplayer functionality, but the current version delivers a polished and engaging experience.
